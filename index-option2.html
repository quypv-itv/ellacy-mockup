Guaranteed Scholarship Program

Secure your education funding with our comprehensive scholarship matching service.

Program Overview

Our Guaranteed Scholarship Program connects you with exclusive funding opportunities matched to your academic profile, achievements, and financial needs. We work with 500+ universities offering merit-based and need-based scholarships.

What's Included

  • Personalized scholarship matching based on your profile
  • Access to scholarships covering up to 100% tuition
  • Application support and document preparation
  • Merit-based and need-based funding options
  • Continuous updates on new scholarship opportunities

Eligibility

Open to all students with strong academic records. Minimum GPA requirements vary by scholarship provider.

Ivy Global Program

Specialized support designed specifically for Ivy League and world-renowned institution applications.

Program Overview

The Ivy Global Program is our premium offering designed for students targeting Ivy League universities and other world-renowned institutions. Our counselors are alumni and admission experts from these elite schools.

What's Included

  • Ivy League-specific admission strategies
  • Premium essay coaching with Ivy alumni
  • Holistic profile building and positioning
  • Extracurricular activity guidance
  • Letters of recommendation strategy

Target Schools

Harvard, Yale, Princeton, Stanford, MIT, Oxford, Cambridge, and other top-tier global institutions.

Joint Program

Explore dual-degree and joint-program opportunities with partner institutions worldwide.

Program Overview

The Joint Program opens doors to unique educational pathways including dual degrees, exchange programs, and collaborative programs between multiple prestigious institutions.

What's Included

  • Access to dual-degree program opportunities
  • International exchange program coordination
  • Partner university network connections
  • Credit transfer and curriculum planning support
  • Cultural preparation and integration assistance

Benefits

Earn degrees from multiple institutions, gain international experience, and expand your global network while studying.

About ASU

Angelo State University is a public university in San Angelo, Texas. Founded in 1928, ASU offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across six colleges. The university is known for its strong academic programs, dedicated faculty, and vibrant campus life.

Fees & Financial Information

Tuition & Fees

Application Fee $50
Tuition (per year) $25,000 - $30,000
Books & Supplies $400

Scholarship Opportunity

We offer generous scholarships up to $30,000 for qualified students. The scholarship amount is determined based on:

  • Academic achievements
  • Extracurricular activities
  • Leadership experience

Academic Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5 on a 4.0 scale
  • English language proficiency (TOEFL iBT 61+/IELTS 5.5+)
  • Official transcripts

Application Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Application fee: $50
  • Personal statement (500 words)
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Copy of passport

Program Overview

The 1+3 Business program allows students to complete their first year of study at a partner institution before transferring to ASU for the remaining three years. This program provides a smooth transition to U.S. higher education while ensuring all credits transfer seamlessly.

Year 1 (Home Country)

  • 1 English Composition I & II
  • 2 College Algebra
  • 3 Introduction to Business
  • 4 Principles of Economics
  • 5 General Education electives

Years 2-4 (ASU)

Students will complete their remaining business degree requirements at ASU's main campus, choosing from various concentrations:

Accounting Finance Marketing Management International Business

About Niagara University

Founded in 1856, Niagara University is a private Catholic university located in Lewiston, New York. The university is known for its strong business programs and commitment to student success, offering a personalized education with small class sizes and dedicated faculty.

Fees & Financial Information

Tuition & Fees

Application Fee $50
Tuition (per year) $35,000 - $40,000
Books & Supplies $500

Scholarship Opportunity

We offer generous scholarships up to $35,000 for qualified students. The scholarship amount is determined based on:

  • Academic excellence
  • Standardized test scores
  • Extracurricular involvement

Academic Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale
  • English language proficiency (TOEFL iBT 79+/IELTS 6.0+)
  • Official transcripts (translated if not in English)

Application Documents

  • Completed online application
  • Non-refundable application fee: $50
  • Personal statement (500-700 words)
  • Two academic letters of recommendation
  • Copy of passport information page

Program Overview

The 2+3 Business Analytics program at Niagara University is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in business principles and advanced analytical skills. The first year is completed at a partner institution, followed by three years of specialized study at Niagara University's College of Business Administration.

Year 1-2 (Home Country)

  • 1 Academic Writing & Research
  • 2 Calculus for Business
  • 3 Introduction to Business Analytics
  • 4 Principles of Management
  • 5 General Education requirements

Years 3-4 (Niagara University)

Students will complete specialized courses in business analytics, including:

Data Mining
Predictive Analytics
Business Intelligence
Data Visualization
Machine Learning
Big Data Analytics

1+3

What is 1+3 Program?

The 1+3 Transfer Program allows students to complete their first year of university studies in Vietnam, then transfer to complete the remaining 3 years at a partner university abroad.

📚 Year 1
Vietnam
🌍 Years 2-4
Overseas
Graduate with international degree
Year 1 (Vietnam)

Foundation studies, English prep, and general education at partner campus

Years 2-4 (Overseas)

Continue major courses at an international university and graduate with a full degree

Credit Transfer

~30 credits from VN year are evaluated and transferred according to articulation

Support & Services

Course mapping & articulation advising

Visa guidance and document review

Housing recommendations and arrival orientation

Why Choose 1+3 Program?

Cost Effective

Save significantly on first-year tuition and living costs

Gradual Transition

Adjust to international education while staying close to family

Language Preparation

Intensive English training before going abroad

Support Network

Travel with Vietnamese classmates; buddy system in the first semester abroad

Entry Flexibility

Suitable for students building GPA/English to meet direct-entry thresholds

Scholarships

Eligible for transfer/merit scholarships at many partner universities

What is 2+2 Program?

The 2+2 Transfer Program allows students to complete their first two years of university studies in Vietnam, then transfer to complete the remaining 2 years at a partner university abroad.

📚 Years 1-2
Vietnam
🌍 Years 3-4
Overseas
Graduate with international degree
Years 1-2 (Vietnam)

Complete core courses and major prerequisites at partner campus in Vietnam

Years 3-4 (Overseas)

Transfer to complete upper-division major courses and graduate with international degree

Credit Transfer

~60 credits from VN years are evaluated and transferred according to articulation

Support & Services

Course mapping & articulation advising

Visa guidance and document review

Housing recommendations and arrival orientation

Why Choose 2+2 Program?

Maximum Savings

Save up to 50% of total education costs

Strong Foundation

Build solid academic base before going abroad

More Preparation Time

Extra year to improve English and adapt to international curriculum

Career Ready

Internship opportunities in Vietnam before studying abroad

Family Support

Stay close to family for two years while building independence

Transfer Scholarships

Eligible for merit scholarships at partner universities

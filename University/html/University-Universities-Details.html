About Angelo State University

Angelo State University, founded in 1928, is a thriving public university in the heart of Texas. As a member of the Texas Tech University System, ASU offers more than 100 majors and concentrations through its six colleges, serving over 10,000 students from across the United States and around the world.

Known for its academic excellence and student success, ASU provides a vibrant campus life with over 150 student organizations, NCAA Division II athletics, and comprehensive academic support services. The university is committed to providing a high-quality, affordable education with small class sizes and personalized attention from faculty.

Located in San Angelo, Texas, ASU's beautiful 268-acre campus features state-of-the-art facilities, modern residence halls, and a welcoming community that fosters student success both in and out of the classroom.

International Pathway Programs

1+3 Program

Complete 1 year of foundation studies followed by 3 years of undergraduate degree at Angelo State University.

  • 1 year foundation studies in home country
  • Guanteed progression to year 2 of degree at ASU
  • Wide range of undergraduate majors available
  • Academic and English language support
Entry Requirements
  • • High School Diploma (GPA 2.5+)
  • • English: IELTS 5.5 or equivalent
  • • Math requirement for STEM programs
  • • Interview may be required

2+2 Program

Complete 2 years at a partner institution followed by 2 years at Angelo State University to earn a bachelor's degree.

  • First 2 years at partner institution
  • Final 2 years at ASU
  • Credit transfer agreement in place
  • Dedicated transfer advisor
Entry Requirements
  • • Completion of 60+ transferable credits
  • • Minimum GPA: 2.5/4.0
  • • English: IELTS 6.0 or equivalent
  • • Course-by-course evaluation

Joint MBA Program

Earn an MBA from Angelo State University through our partnership with select international institutions.

  • Complete foundation courses at partner institution
  • Finish MBA coursework at ASU
  • Complete in as little as 1 year
  • Specializations available in various business fields
Entry Requirements
  • • Bachelor's degree or equivalent
  • • Minimum GPA: 3.0/4.0
  • • English: IELTS 6.5 or equivalent
  • • 2+ years work experience preferred
  • • GMAT/GRE may be required

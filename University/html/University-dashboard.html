Pathway • 1+3

Ellacy Transfer Program (1+3)

Start your first year in Vietnam, then transfer to a partner university abroad to complete the final 3 years. Build academic skills and English locally, reduce initial costs, and transition smoothly with full advising support.

Includes credit evaluation, course mapping, and visa guidance. Graduate with an international degree after upper-division coursework overseas.

Pathway • 2+2

Ellacy Transfer Program (2+2)

Study 2 years in Vietnam to complete core and prerequisite courses, then transfer to finish the last 2 years abroad. Gain academic maturity, improve English, and plan finances before moving.

Focus on upper-division major courses overseas with fewer repeats. Advising, credit mapping, and visa support are provided throughout.

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the difference between P3A, P3B, and P3C?
P3A (Standard) covers full application & essay mentoring to 5–10 Top 30 US universities. P3B (VIP Career) adds career coaching, CV/LinkedIn, and internship mentoring. P3C (President) includes all VIP features plus guided campus visits and leadership mentoring.
Do you guarantee scholarships or admissions?
Ivy Global focuses on Top 30 US schools; outcomes depend on each student’s profile. We provide intensive coaching to maximize results, but admissions and scholarships are determined by universities.
Is the fee refundable?
No. Ivy Global packages are non-refundable once work has started.

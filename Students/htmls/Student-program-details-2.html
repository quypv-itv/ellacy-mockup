About Tiffin University

Tiffin University is a private university located in Tiffin, Ohio. Founded in 1888, TU serves over 3,000 students and is committed to transforming lives through education. The university is known for its personalized attention with a 20:1 student-faculty ratio and diverse academic programs.

Follow a path. Blaze a trail. Tiffin University offers 50+ undergraduate and graduate programs across various schools including Business, Criminal Justice & Social Sciences, Science Technology & Health, and Arts Education & Humanities. The university is committed to providing challenging academic programs that enhance global competencies and skills for success in a diverse world.

Located in Tiffin, Ohio, students benefit from a close-knit campus community and personalized educational experience. With 98% of students receiving financial aid and 50+ student clubs and organizations, TU provides comprehensive support for student success and personal growth.

Transfer Programs (1+3 & 2+2)

What is 1+3 Program?

The 1+3 Transfer Program allows students to complete their first year of university studies in Vietnam, then transfer to complete the remaining 3 years at a partner university abroad.

  • Year 1: Foundation studies in Vietnam with English preparation
  • Years 2-4: Complete bachelor's degree at international university
  • Credits: All Vietnamese credits transfer seamlessly

Why Choose 1+3 Program?

  • Cost Effective: Save significantly on first-year tuition and living costs
  • Gradual Transition: Adjust to international education while staying close to family
  • Language Preparation: Intensive English training before going abroad
  • Support Network: Travel with Vietnamese classmates for mutual support

What is 2+2 Program?

The 2+2 Transfer Program allows students to complete their first two years of university studies in Vietnam, then transfer to complete the final 2 years at a partner university abroad.

  • Years 1-2: Core curriculum and major prerequisites in Vietnam
  • Years 3-4: Advanced major courses at international university
  • Credits: 60+ credits transfer with advanced standing

Why Choose 2+2 Program?

  • Maximum Savings: Save up to 50% of total education costs
  • Academic Maturity: Build strong foundation before international study
  • Cultural Readiness: More time to prepare for cultural adaptation
  • Focused Study: Concentrate on advanced major courses abroad

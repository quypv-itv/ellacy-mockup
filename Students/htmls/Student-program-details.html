About University of Berkeley

The University of Berkeley is Canada's leading institution of higher learning and research. Founded in 1827, it is consistently ranked as one of the top universities globally and is renowned for the excellence of its research, teaching, and innovation.

UofT offers world-class engineering programs with cutting-edge research facilities and strong industry connections. The Faculty of Applied Science & Engineering is home to nine departments offering undergraduate and graduate programs in various engineering disciplines including mechanical, electrical, computer, civil, and chemical engineering.

Located in downtown Berkeley, students benefit from being in Canada's largest city and financial center. The university maintains strong partnerships with industry leaders and provides excellent opportunities for internships, co-op programs, and career development.

IVY Global Preparation Program

Dreaming of Harvard, Yale, Stanford, or MIT? Our IVY Global Preparation Program is designed specifically for ambitious students targeting the Ivy League and Top 30 global universities.

We provide comprehensive end-to-end consulting, from building a standout academic profile to strategic essay guidance, mock interviews, and scholarship planning. Our proven methodology and personalized mentorship from Ivy League alumni give you the competitive edge needed for admission to the world's most prestigious institutions.

Elite Admission Program

Aiming for the Top 100 universities worldwide? Our Elite Admission Program optimizes every aspect of your application to help you stand out in a competitive landscape.

From crafting compelling personal statements to strategic test preparation and interview coaching, we ensure your unique story resonates with admission committees. Our data-driven approach combined with personalized 1-on-1 mentoring from senior advisors maximizes your chances at prestigious institutions like Butler University, Ohio Northern, and other highly-ranked schools.

Guaranteed Scholarship Program

Worried about financing your education abroad? Our Guaranteed Scholarship Program ensures you receive 2-3 scholarship offers with comprehensive financial and visa support.

We remove financial barriers by securing multiple funding opportunities from our network of 100+ partner universities, including exclusive partnerships like Angelo State University. Beyond scholarships, we provide end-to-end support including financial planning, loan guidance, and visa application assistance with a >95% success rate. Study abroad shouldn't be just for the wealthy—let us help you unlock global education opportunities.

Joint Program

Looking for a smart, cost-effective pathway to a U.S. degree? Our Joint Program lets you start in Vietnam and seamlessly transfer to top American universities.

This strategic 2+2 approach significantly reduces tuition costs by approximately 40% while maintaining quality education and ensuring credit transfer. Study your first 1-2 years at partner institutions in Vietnam (Banking Academy, UEH, Van Lang University), then transfer to complete your degree at prestigious U.S. universities. Perfect for families seeking an affordable yet prestigious American degree with full support throughout your academic journey and visa transition.

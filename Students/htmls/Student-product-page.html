Four Pathways to Your Future

Guaranteed Scholarship Program
Description

Students apply to 3–5 universities (mainly within Ellacy’s network, such as Angelo State, Ohio Northern, Butler University). Ellacy guarantees 2–3 scholarship offers, provides complete application and visa support, and assists with documentation and personal statements.

Key Benefits
  • Guaranteed Outcome: 2–3 scholarship offers secured for each applicant.
  • High Success Rate: >95% visa success through Ellacy’s consulting process.
  • Comprehensive Support: Guidance from application, essay writing, to admission and visa interview.
  • Financial Protection: 100% refund if no scholarship is received (except in case of falsified documents).
  • Network Advantage: Access to 100+ partner universities and high-value scholarships (up to 100% tuition).

Elite Admission Program

Description

An upgraded version of the Guaranteed Scholarship Program. Students apply to 3–5 top-tier schools and receive personalized mentorship from Ellacy’s senior advisors. The program emphasizes profile optimization, essay enhancement, and interview readiness.

Key Benefits
  • Higher Scholarship Tiers: Tailored application strategy for high-ranking universities.
  • Elite Mentorship: 1-on-1 coaching on academic and personal branding.
  • Application Refinement: Intensive support on essays, recommendation letters, and documentation.
  • Visa & Admission Assurance: >95% visa success, strong admission support from Ellacy’s U.S. and Vietnam offices.
  • Professional Refund Policy: Full refund if no scholarship is offered after verified application process.

IVY Global Preparation Program
Description

Targeted at ambitious students applying to 7–10 Top 30 U.S. & Canadian universities, focusing on Ivy League and elite institutions. It includes end-to-end consulting, essay guidance, mock interviews, and scholarship strategy planning.

  • Comprehensive Visa Support: Proven 95%+ visa success rate.

Joint Program

Description

Study the first 1–2 years in Vietnam, then transfer to the US to complete your degree. The program enables students to save costs, gain international study experience, and receive full guidance on credit transfer, English readiness, and visa application through Ellacy’s network of partner universities.

Key Benefits
  • Cost Savings: ~40% lower compared to studying all 4 years in the US.
  • Smooth Transfer: Guaranteed credit transfer & dedicated visa assistance.
  • Academic Readiness: English and academic preparation before departure.
  • Local Partners: Banking Academy, UEH, Van Lang University.
  • Global Advantage: Eligible for additional Ellacy scholarships after transfer.

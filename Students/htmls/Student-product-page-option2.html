Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our programs

What does the +10% scholarship fee mean?

The 10% fee is calculated on the total scholarship value you receive. For example: if you receive a $20,000 scholarship, the service fee will be $2,000. This fee is only charged after you successfully receive the scholarship.

What is the refund policy if visa is denied?

If your visa application is rejected due to our error, you will receive a 100% refund of the service fee. We are committed to maximum support to ensure a 98% visa success rate.

Can I change schools?

Yes, you can change schools in the early stages of the application process. However, changing schools may affect the timeline and requires advisor approval to ensure it aligns with your profile.

How long does application processing take?

Average processing time is 8-12 weeks depending on the service package and school you choose. Elite and Ivy packages may take 12-16 weeks due to more complex processes.
